    Marketplace

    The Marketplace is the meeting place for sellers and buyers. Here you can display the items in your inventory that you wish to sell or trade. If you would like to add an item to the Marketplace, see the article put an item up for sale

    How do I list an item for sale?

    When you add an item to your inventory, you have the option to publish it on the marketplace. All Abelaa users can view the listing and buy or trade your item.

    The contents of your inventory are confidential until you decide to list it for sale, but users will be able to see all the items you list on the marketplace.

    From your inventory or the item page:

    1. Click on “List for Sale” or “List in Marketplace”.
      • A modal window will open with a short form.
    2. Fill in the required information. You must provide the following:
      • Quantity: Indicate the number of units you wish to sell or trade.
      • Seller's WhatsApp Number: Enter the number where buyers can contact you.
      • Location: Indicate the location of the item.
      • Type of Exchange: Sale (item for cash), Trade (item for item), Mixed (either one or both)
    3. Click on “List for Sale”.

    Your item is now visible in the marketplace to all platform users.

    How ​​to view the Marketplace?

    You can view a company's or individual's products in the Marketplace, accessible via the Market option on the home screen or from the dashboard's quick actions. You can also find your items there if you have published any.

    View an item

    1. Open the Marketplace.
    2. Tap view details.

    How ​​do I search for an item in the Marketplace?

    In the Marketplace, you can search for an item or type of item in order to purchase it. You can use the search bar in the Marketplace area.

    How ​​to Search for an Item in the Marketplace?

    The Listing Search feature allows you to quickly find items available on the marketplace or within a specific Inventory Group. With the search bar, advanced filters, and smart sorting, you can easily identify the most relevant items for you.

    Accessing Marketplace Listings

    To view listings for a group:

    1. Open the Marketplace section in the app.

    2. Select an Inventory Group.

    3. The listings available for that group will automatically appear.

    Each group contains listings published by its members or administrators.

    Search by Keyword

    The search feature allows you to search for an item to buy using a keyword. Perform a keyword search in the marketplace.

    1. Click on the Search bar. 2. Enter the word you are looking for in the search field.

    The list of matching products will be displayed at the bottom.

    Filters

    Adding a filter allows you to search for one or more items with common attributes. Filters are located to the right of the search bar. You can apply one or more filters by selecting their values.

    1. Filter by Location
      Filter listings based on the location specified by the seller.

      • Only one location can be selected at a time.
      • Only listings corresponding to that location will be displayed.

    2. Filter by Transaction Type
      Choose the transaction type offered:

      • Sale – the item is sold for cash
      • Trade – exchange for another item
      • Mixed – sale or trade possible

    3. Filter by Category
      Filter by item category:

      • Electronics
      • Home & Furniture
      • Clothing
      • Miscellaneous Items
      • Vehicles
      • And other available categories

    4. Filter by Subcategory

      Further refine your results by selecting a subcategory.

      Examples:

      • Under Electronics: Phones, Smartwatches, Computers
      • Under Items: Bags, Shoes, Accessories

    Note: If you are unable to find your item in the Marketplace, please contact customer service.

    About Purchasing and Trading

    In the Marketplace, you can choose to either trade or buy.

    How ​​do I buy an item?

    When you buy, you pay a pre-agreed amount to obtain someone else's item.

    1. Open the Marketplace
    2. Locate the item you want to obtain
    3. Press trade > buy > submit

    How ​​do I trade one item for another?

    Unlike buying, bartering requires you to have items in your inventory to exchange them for another.

    1. Open the Marketplace
    2. Locate the item you want to obtain
    3. Press trade > Make a trade
    4. Enter the name of the item you want to give in exchange and select it.
    5. Click submit

    Note

    If the trade is accepted by the other party, you will receive a notification.

    How ​​do I edit one of my items? ?

    To edit one of your items, you can either view details of your item in the MarketPlace and press the Edit button at the bottom. Or you can edit it in inventory.

    How ​​do I remove a product from the Marketplace?

    If you've changed your mind and decided to no longer sell your item, you can remove it from the Marketplace.

    1. Open the Marketplace.
    2. Search for your product in the Marketplace.
    3. Tap View details
    4. Disable the published option and confirm.

    ASSET LISTING MANAGEMENT

    Our marketplace allows users to list and sell their physical assets by creating individual listings.

    What is an Asset Listing?

    An Asset represents the physical object itself.

    An Asset Listing is a specific offer made by a seller for an asset.
    Multiple users can post listings for the same asset, and a seller can even create multiple listings with different terms.
    Therefore, the same asset can have one or more listings, each processed independently.

    Each listing is displayed separately on the marketplace, even if they are for the same asset.

    Example of an Ad Card

    Item: iPhone 12
    Seller Location: Douala
    Quantity Available: 1
    Transaction Type: Sale
    Seller WhatsApp: 678 000 111
    Wanted: Negotiable
    Estimated Value: 150,000 XAF

    How ​​do I get my ad to appear?

    Ads appear if they meet these conditions:

    • The item is published.
    • The item status is commercial.
    • The item is of the physical type.
    • The item has a valid location.
    • The ad has at least one unit available (quantity – quantity ordered ≥ 1).
    • The asset and the listing are not deleted (soft delete).

    About Listing Ranking

    The marketplace ranks listings according to the following criteria:

    • Assets recently viewed by the user are prioritized.
    • Assets from sellers with similar business areas appear next.
    • Other listings are then displayed, sorted by their publication date.

    This ensures a more relevant experience for each user.

    Listing Availability

    Each listing includes three values:

    • Quantity: total number of units added by the seller
    • Quantity Ordered: units already reserved or in the process of being purchased
    • Available Quantity = Quantity – Quantity Ordered

    When the available quantity reaches 0, the listing is automatically hidden.

    How ​​Do Buyers Interact?

    Buyers can:

    • View all listings for the same item
    • Contact the seller via WhatsApp
    • Place orders based on available quantity
    • Read the specific details of each listing

    Each listing is completely independent. Ordering from one listing does not affect the others.

    When Should You Create Multiple Listings?

    A seller should create multiple listings when:

    • The product is available in different cities
    • They want to offer different prices
    • They offer different types of transactions
    • They want to target different groups of buyers
    • They have multiple units sold separately
