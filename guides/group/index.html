    Inventory Groups

    An inventory group is a space where you can group your items according to your own criteria.
    To access the inventory group, you must, from one of the application's features, click on Inventory Group in the menu.

    How ​​to create an inventory group?

    To create an inventory group:

    1. Click on Inventory Groups.
    2. Click on New Inventory Group.
    3. Enter the information for your group, give it a type, and a cover image (optional).
      • Click on Public to make the group visible on the Marketplace.
      • Click on Private to make the group visible only to you and the members you have added.
      • Click on Choose File and then select an image from your device to add a cover image.
    4. Click on Create.

    How to search for one or more inventory groups?

    If you have multiple inventory groups, you may want to perform operations on one or more of them. To make this easier, simply: Go to the search bar and type the name or part of the description of the group(s) concerned. Your search results will appear instantly in the top bar.

    How to add an item to the inventory group?

    You can add as many items as you want to a group if you are the administrator or if the administrators have authorized all members to add items.

    1. Find the relevant inventory group.
    2. Click on Add item.
    3. Click on Choose from inventory to add an item that already exists in your inventory. Simply type the name and then click on the item.
    4. Click on Add asset to create a new item directly in the inventory group and fill in the item's information.
    5. Confirm the creation or addition of the item.

    Note: Items added from the inventory group will be automatically added to the inventory.

    How to change the status of a group?

    The use or role of an inventory group is defined by the person who creates it. This person can also decide whether other users should have access to their inventory group or not. They also have the right to change their mind at any time.

    To change the status of an inventory group (private to public or public to private):

    1. Find the relevant inventory group and click on it.
    2. Click on the three dots on the right.
    3. Click on Make private to make it private.
    4. Click on Make public to make it public.

    How to modify an inventory group?

    If you want to edit information about one of your inventory groups:

    1. Search for the inventory group, then click on the group name. 2. Click on the three vertical dots.
    2. Select Edit, and make any necessary changes.
    3. Click Save.

    How to delete an inventory group?

    You can delete a group for all group members if you are the administrator (manager). Before you can delete the group, you must remove it from the Marketplace if it is listed there. When you delete a group, it will no longer be visible in your list of inventory groups, but the items it contained will remain visible in your inventory.

    1. Go to the group and make it private if it isn't already.
    2. Click on the three dots on the right side of the screen and then tap delete.
    3. Confirm the deletion.

    Add user to inventor group

    The Add User to Inventory Group feature allows enterprise owners and group administrators to add members to specific inventory groups within the platform.
    This enables teams to collaborate on inventory management, assign roles, and control access to resources more effectively.

    There are two ways to add a user to an inventory group.

    Method 1: Create a New User

    1. Open the inventory group to which you want to add a member.
    2. Click the ⋮ (More options) button, then select Add a member.
    3. In the modal window that opens, choose Create a new user.
    4. Enter the following information:
      • First Name
      • Last Name
      • Email Address
      • Role (Administrator / Member / Viewer) Click Save.

    Method 2: Add an Existing User

    1. Open the relevant inventory group.
    2. Click ⋮ (More options) then select Add a member.
    3. In the modal window, choose Select an existing user.
    4. In the search bar, enter the email, first name, or last name of the user you want to add.
    5. The system will automatically start searching as you type (real-time search).
    6. Browse the displayed results and click Add next to the desired user.
    7. Once successfully added, the user will appear in the group's member list.

    Note

    • A user cannot be added to the same inventory group twice.
    • Only business owners or group administrators can perform this action.
