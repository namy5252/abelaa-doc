# Inventory Groups

An inventory group is a space where you can group your items according to your own criteria.<br>
To access the inventory group, you must, from one of the application's features, click on ***Inventory Group*** in the menu.

## **How ​​to create an inventory group?**

To create an inventory group:

1. Click on ***Inventory Groups***.
2. Click on **New Inventory Group**.
3. Enter the information for your group, give it a type, and a cover image (**optional**). 
    - Click on ***Public*** to make the group visible on the Marketplace. 
    - Click on ***Private*** to make the group visible only to you and the members you have added. 
    - Click on ***Choose File*** and then select an image from your device to add a **cover image**.
4. Click on ***Create***.

## How to search for one or more inventory groups?

If you have multiple inventory groups, you may want to perform operations on one or more of them. To make this easier, simply:
Go to the search bar and type the name or part of the description of the group(s) concerned. Your search results will appear instantly in the top bar.

## How to add an item to the inventory group?

You can add as many items as you want to a group if you are the administrator or if the administrators have authorized all members to add items.

1. Find the relevant inventory group.
2. Click on ***Add item***.
- Click on **Choose from inventory** to add an item that already exists in your inventory. Simply type the name and then click on the item.
- Click on **Add asset** to create a new item directly in the inventory group and fill in the item's information.
3. Confirm the creation or addition of the item.

> Note:
> Items added from the inventory group will be automatically added to the inventory.

## How to change the status of a group?

The use or role of an inventory group is defined by the person who creates it. This person can also decide whether other users should have access to their inventory group or not. They also have the right to change their mind at any time.

To change the status of an inventory group (private to public or public to private):

1. Find the relevant inventory group and click on it.
2. Click on the three dots on the right.
- Click on **Make private** to make it private.
- Click on **Make public** to make it public.

## How to modify an inventory group? 

If you want to edit information about one of your inventory groups:

1. Search for the inventory group, then click on the group name. 2. Click on the three vertical dots.
3. Select **Edit**, and make any necessary changes.
4. Click **Save**.

## How to delete an inventory group?

You can delete a group for all group members if you are the administrator (manager). Before you can delete the group, you must remove it from the Marketplace if it is listed there.
When you delete a group, it will no longer be visible in your list of inventory groups, but the items it contained will remain visible in your inventory.

1. Go to the group and [make it private](#how-to-change-the-status-of-a-group) if it isn't already.
2. Click on the three dots on the right side of the screen and then tap **delete**.
3. Confirm the deletion.
